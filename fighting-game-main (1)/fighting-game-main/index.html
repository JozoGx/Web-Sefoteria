Meet the


Characters

In the depths of the ancient forest, three creatures of myth and legend dwell. There is the ancient forest guardian, a spider with a glowing eye and a sprawling web, said to be an evil spirit trapped in its form. Its power is immense, a secret held within its single eye. Then there is the little cobra, seemingly adorable but cunning and deadly. Its whistling lure and venomous bite make it a formidable predator. Lastly, the Lizardman, a grumpy dragon warrior with bright blue skin and sharp scales. It guards the underground treasures, its strength and agility making it a deadly foe. These three creatures, each with its own unique abilities and history, have long been part of the forest's lore, their existence a testament to the ancient and mysterious forces that shape the world.

Seraphina

With her shimmering green hair and eyes, and a captivating smile, Princess Seraphina is the jewel of the Wedness Kingdom. Her beauty is striking, but her intelligence and kindness shine even brighter. She is a wise leader, beloved by her people. Behind her golden crown lies a longing for adventure and freedom.
